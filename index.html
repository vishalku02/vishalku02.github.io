Papers

DiagramIR: An Automatic Pipeline for Educational Math Diagram Evaluation

Vishal Kumar, Shubhra Mishra, Rebecca Hao, Rizwaan Malik, David Broman, Dorottya Demszky

NeurIPS Math-AI Workshop 2025

DiagramIR introduces a scalable method for automatic evaluation of diagram quality when no ground truth exists. The method achieves higher agreement with human raters and enables smaller models to match frontier ones at a fraction of the inference cost.

GitHub

AmpleGCG-Plus: A Strong Generative Model of Adversarial Suffixes to Jailbreak LLMs with Higher Success Rates in Fewer Attempts

Vishal Kumar*, Zeyi Liao*, Jaylen Jones, Huan Sun

COLM 2024 [poster]

AmpleGCG-Plus is a suite of LLMs for rapidly "jailbreaking" other LLMs. Based on Ample-GCG, our models learn the GCG jailbreaking algorithm and generalize to any harmful query, enabling scalable automatic red-teaming.

HuggingFace

Somm: A wine recommendation engine

Palak Arora, Pavel Kovalev, Vishal Kumar, Veronica Miaatto, Jade Nguyen

Erdos Institute Data Science Bootcamp Showcase

Somm helps new wine drinkers navigate the wine market via simple queires about desired flavor notes. We built a retrieval and re-ranking algorithm over expert wine reviews. The project won a top prize among 500+ students at the Erdos Institute.
