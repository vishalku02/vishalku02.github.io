Papers

DiagramIR: An Automatic Pipeline for Educational Math Diagram Evaluation

Vishal Kumar, Shubhra Mishra, Rebecca Hao, Rizwaan Malik, David Broman, Dorottya Demszky

NeurIPS Math-AI Workshop 2025

DiagramIR introduces a scalable method for evaluating math diagrams when no ground truth exists. The pipeline translates TikZ code into an intermediate representation where geometric constraints can be applied, improving agreement with human raters and letting smaller models match larger systems at a fraction of the inference cost.

AmpleGCG-Plus: A Strong Generative Model of Adversarial Suffixes to Jailbreak LLMs with Higher Success Rates in Fewer Attempts

Vishal Kumar*, Zeyi Liao*, Jaylen Jones, Huan Sun

COLM 2024 [poster]

AmpleGCG-Plus is a suite of LLMs for rapidly “jailbreaking” other LLMs. Based on Ample-GCG, our models learn the GCG jailbreak algorithm and generalize to arbitrary harmful prompts, enabling fast robustness tests.

Somm: A wine recommendation engine

Palak Arora, Pavel Kovalev, Vishal Kumar, Veronica Miaatto, Jade Nguyen

Erdos Institute Data Science Bootcamp Showcase

Somm helps new wine drinkers find bottles using RAG with GPT-4 and Cohere Rerank to translate flavor descriptions into wine suggestions. The project won a top prize among 500+ students at the Erdos Institute.
